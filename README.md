<h1 align="center">FBx - Clean Facebook Feed</h1>

<p align="center">🧩 Facebook without the noise. No ads, no reels, just what you want to see.</p>

## Main Features

FBx removes ads and distractions from Facebook for a clean, focused news feed. Every feature can be toggled on or off from the extension popup. Hide what you want, keep what you like.

- **Hide Sponsored Posts (Ads Blocker for Facebook)**: automatically detects and removes sponsored posts from your news feed.
- **Hide Reels**: removes the Reels section from your feed so short-form videos stop hijacking your scroll.
- **Hide Stories**: hides the Stories bar at the top of your news feed for a cleaner homepage.
- **Hide Sidebar Ads**: removes sponsored banners from the right-hand sidebar.

Lightweight and private: no data collection, no tracking, no external requests. Everything runs locally in your browser.

## Keeping up with Facebook

Facebook changes its page structure from time to time, which can temporarily break some features. When that happens, an update usually follows within a day or two. If something stops working, make sure you are on the latest version, or open an issue to report it.
